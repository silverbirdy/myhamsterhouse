Privacy Policy

My Hamster House

Effective date: March 5, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 5, 2026

My Hamster House ("we", "our", or "the app") is a casual simulation game developed by Eunse Shin. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

My Hamster House is designed to be played fully offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, location, etc.).

All game data (hamsters, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

My Hamster House uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data to serve relevant advertisements, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA / IDFV), subject to your ATT consent
  • IP address and general location (country/region)
  • Device type, OS version, and app usage data

We request your consent before enabling personalized ads via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases

My Hamster House offers optional in-app purchases via Apple's StoreKit. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

See Apple's Privacy Policy for details on purchase data.

4. Children's Privacy

My Hamster House is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no retention period to define. Game data resides solely on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or California, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising at any time via your device settings
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: silverbird.dev@gmail.com
